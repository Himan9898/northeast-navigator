Who We Are

Born in the Northeast,
for the Northeast

Northeast Navigator is a Guwahati-based travel company built by people who grew up here — who know the difference between tourist spots and real experiences. We've spent over a decade mapping the Northeast so you can experience it the way locals do.

We cover all eight states with carefully designed itineraries for every kind of traveller. Our local guides are natives of each state, ensuring you experience authentic stories, not packaged performances.

We believe the Northeast is India's most extraordinary region — and one of the world's most underrated destinations. Our mission is to share it while supporting local communities and preserving fragile ecosystems.

Local Expertise
Every guide is a native of the state they lead in. Real knowledge, real stories.
Small Groups
Maximum 12 per group. Personal attention and minimal environmental impact.
Responsible Tourism
We partner with local homestays, tribal artisans, and community cooperatives.
Permit Support
We handle all Inner Line Permits for Arunachal, Nagaland, and Manipur.

What We Offer

Experiences That
Define You

🦏
Wildlife Safaris
Jeep safaris through Kaziranga, Manas & Namdapha. Track one-horned rhinos, Royal Bengal tigers & rare clouded leopards at dawn.
🏔️
Mountain Treks
Trek Dzongri, Goecha La & remote Mechuka Valley. Himalayan vistas that will permanently reset your perspective on life.

Cultural Immersion
Live with Naga tribes, witness Bihu dances, attend Hornbill & Sangai festivals. Culture is our most powerful destination.

Offbeat Trails
Floating villages, zero-light skies, living root bridges. The Northeast holds India's most untouched road-less-travelled corners.

About Us

Your Guide to the Northeast

Northeast Navigator was founded in Guwahati by third-generation locals who grew up trekking these forests and attending tribal festivals. Every itinerary is designed by people with lived experience of these roads, rivers, and cultures.

  • Government-approved & IATA-certified travel operator
  • In-house naturalists, cultural guides & trek leaders
  • 24/7 local support — our team lives where you visit
  • Responsible tourism that gives back to local communities
  • Fully customizable for every budget & group size
